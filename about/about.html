About Me

In addition to cybersecurity training, I have experience developing production-style web applications for the life coaching industry, including secure authentication workflows, administrative dashboards, payment integrations, scheduling systems, readiness assessments, analytics reporting, and AI-assisted monitoring features. My development workflow combines modern AI-assisted engineering practices with hands-on debugging, frontend/backend integration, and secure application design.

For the past six years, I’ve worked in the youth residential care industry, where I’ve been responsible for ensuring client safety, maintaining strict confidentiality, writing detailed incident reports, and applying crisis prevention and intervention strategies. For the past two (2) years I have dedicated my time and focus to full-stack web development and the praxis of implementing security frameworks for a more secure workflow environment.

These experiences strengthened my situational awareness, analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and ability to remain calm under pressure—skills that now directly support my work in software development and cybersecurity. I’m passionate about building secure, scalable systems that improve operational workflows while continuing to grow as a software engineer with a strong security-oriented mindset.
